About

Eager and willing to embark on a new carrer, Max has recently emerged from the Coding Boot Camp at University of Oregon with thourough knowlege in HTML, CSS and Java Script (pending). Until now, he has made his living in the world of Bluegrass Banjo: performing live throughout the world, organizing festivals, teaching lessons and building instruments. Max has a strong draw to create and this has manifested itself in his building of websites (up until now, self-taught) for not only his bands, but also for the Bridgetown Bluegrass Festival, which he co-founded and continues to organize. Max is excited to continue and expand his website building skills in the next chapter of his life.
